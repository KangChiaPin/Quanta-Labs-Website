Quanta's Experience

With this variety of special types of test equipment, we are able to test many different products to validate their performance in differing environments. We have helped companies meet office noise specifications with our acoustic chamber and provide validation for products to survive harsh ocean conditions with our salt spray chamber. We have also developed in-house fiber optic intensity tests which include the four basic tests of flex, twist, side-pull and cable retention, all computer controlled to validate component fiber physical integrity.
