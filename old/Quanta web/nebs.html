Quanta's Capabilities

● Capable of handling all NEBS Testing
● All operational NEBS Testing done on-site
● Newly installed seismic shaker and state-of-the-art control room
● Video camera to record all tests
● Large seismic test system capable of handling heavy telecom racks
● Quick turnaround on reports, accepted by all major telecom companies
● Audit approval by UL

Purpose

Demonstrate compliance with Telcordia CR-63 core generic requirements to have a minimum criteria for telecommunication equipment systems for all Central Office and Local Exchange Carriers.

Quanta's Experience

Quanta Laboratories has worked with many network equipment and optical equipment companies and several testing partners to provide one-stop NEBS testing and reporting to all the major telecom companies. Quanta has installed the latest Seismic Test System and a state-of-the-art sound proof control room where you can observe your test in a comfortable environment.
